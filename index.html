2. Age Restrictions and Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically designed for or directed at children under the age of 13.

The App is rated PEGI 7 and is intended for general audiences aged 7 and older. While children may use the App, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at shapeinc25@gmail.com.

For users in the United States: We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). For more information about COPPA, please visit the Federal Trade Commission's website at https://www.ftc.gov/tips-advice/business-center/privacy-and-security/children%27s-privacy.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play the Game. You can enjoy the full gameplay experience without entering any personal data.

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain technical information through third-party services:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, device identifiers (such as Advertising ID on Android and IDFA on iOS)
  • App Usage Data: App version, time zone, locale/language settings, session duration, time spent in the app, app launches, and player retention metrics
  • In-App Purchase Information: Records of purchases made within the App (such as ad removal or donation transactions)
  • Advertising Data: Information necessary to serve advertisements, including Advertising ID

3.3 Game Progress Data

All game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. We do not store your game progress on our servers or in the cloud.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Analytics: To understand how users interact with the App, measure player retention, improve gameplay experience, and fix bugs
  • Advertising: To display advertisements within the App
  • In-App Purchases: To process and fulfill your purchases (ad removal, donations)
  • App Improvement: To analyze app performance, identify technical issues, and develop new features

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information about you:

5.1 Google Analytics for Firebase

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to collect usage statistics and analytics data.

  • Data Collected: Device information, app usage patterns, session data, crash reports
  • Purpose: To understand user behavior and improve the App
  • Data Retention: Analytics data is retained for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.2 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App.

  • Data Collected: Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android), device information, IP address, ad interaction data
  • Purpose: To serve personalized advertisements based on your interests
  • Ad Personalization: The App displays personalized ads using your Advertising ID to show relevant advertisements
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • How to Opt-Out of Personalized Ads:
    • Android: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
    • iOS: Go to Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Turn on Limit Ad Tracking

5.3 Google Play Store and Apple App Store

This App is distributed through Google Play Store and Apple App Store, which have their own privacy policies governing app downloads, updates, and in-app purchases:

  • Google Play Store Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

5.4 Ad Removal Feature

Users can choose to remove advertisements by making a one-time in-app purchase. Once purchased:

  • All third-party advertisements will be permanently disabled in the App
  • Google AdMob will no longer collect advertising-related data
  • Analytics data collection will continue for app improvement purposes
